HERO BACKGROUND IMAGE INSTRUCTIONS
===================================

Add your hero background image as: /public/hero-background.jpg

Image Requirements:
- Format: JPG or WebP (preferred for better performance)
- Dimensions: Minimum 1920x1080px (Full HD), 2560x1440px recommended
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 or wider
- File Size: Optimize to under 500KB using tools like TinyPNG or Squoosh
- Subject: Editorial-style fitness photo with a woman in workout attire
- Style: Natural lighting, modern aesthetic, warm tones
- Composition: Subject should be on the right side (content is on the left)

The image will have a dark overlay (charcoal/80 to charcoal/60) applied to ensure text readability.

Alternative filenames supported:
- /public/hero-background.jpg
- /public/hero-background.webp
- /public/hero-background.png

If using a different filename, update line 14 in:
components/sections/Hero.tsx
